SHROUD AND STAY PLATES

The tripple and single chain plates are similar to the Fisher plates and do have the same mounting dimensions. These plates have a flush back, unlike the old standard plates built over the decades. The base is 1/16 inch thick and 5/16 inch wide. The loops will take either the TB1 , TB2, or PB3 pond boat turnbuckle.

LARGE SERIES TEES

A new series of shroud plates is available for larger boats from forty inch on up to "J's" It is machined from a 3/8" by 1/2" bar ans has .156" diameter holes to to take the larger shackle of the PB-2 series turnbuckles. They are available with 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9 holes. The lengths are from 5/8" for the single hole to 3 1/2" long for the nine hole unit.


SP-2 ( SMALLER TEE)

The SP-2 style shroud plates are machined from solid brass stock and offer a more modern look than the Fisher type. The SP-2-Single is 1.00" long, and the SP-2-Tripple is 1.50" long. Both have mounting holes for #2 screws, and the side holes are 0.156" diameter and will accept either the TB1, TB2, or TB2-XL pond boat turnbuckles.

FAIRLEADS


MYF-DFT-1


This deck feed thru fairlead, PN: MYF-DFT-1, is designed to be mounted through your deck using a 1/4 inch hole. This part will mount in deck thicknesses of 1/16 to 3/8 inch. The total fitting length is 1.25 inches long, threading is 1/4-28 and includes the brass nut. The thru hole is 5/64 inch diameter with both ends having radius for smooth sheet travel. The deck side of this item looks simular to a winch and has flats to use a 9/32 inch wrench for holding while the nut is tightened.

P/N: MYF-DFT-1 is priced at $8.75 each.
