Goosenecks

Custom made goosenecks can be machined from brass per your dimensions. The mast and boom diameters must be given for the quote.An approved sketch shall be done before machining. These goosenecks consist of four parts: The boom connection uses brass tube soldered to the boom hinge. The mast band, pin and main hinge complete the assembly. Hardware can be provided.

Please call or email for a quote.

Smaller Flat Mount Gooseneck


The small flat mount gooseneck mounts on a 1/4 inch wide flat area on your mast. The mast mount is 1/4 inch wide by 1.0 inch long and the mast mounting holes are for #2 screws. The boom mounts by drilling a 5/32 diameter hole in the boom and glueing the probe into the boom. Your boom may be faired into the 3/8 inch brass boom diameter or different boom diameters may be ordered. This gooseneck includes a small shackle bolted with #0-80 hex hardware.

PRICE: Is dependant on size and design. Please call or email.

LARGE FLAT MOUNT GOOSENECK


LARGE FLAT MOUNT GOOSENECK


The large flat mount gooseneck mounts on a 3/8 inch wide flat area on your mast. The mast mount is 5/16 inch wide by 1.4 inch long and the mast mounting holes are for # 2 screws. The boom mounts by drilling a 5/32 diameter hole in the boom and glueing the probe into the boom. Your boom may be faired into the 3/8 inch brass boom diameter. Different boom diameters may be ordered. This gooseneck includes a small shackle bolted with #0-80 hex hardware.

Boom Vangs

Various configurations and sizes may be manufactured. The vang length may be modified for your custom use. The picture below shows the three general sizes of vangs. The top unit is about 6 inches long, and used for boats up to 36 inches overall. The mid unit is about 10 inches long, and used for boats up to 56 inches overall. The lower unit is about 15 inches long, and used for BIG boats including "J" boats. The adjustment thread for this unit is #10-32 and uses 5/16 inch rod.



NEW !! ADJUSTABLE BOOM VANG

Now there is an adjustable boom vang to match the flat mount gooseneck. This unit mounts with screws on the mast and round head screws on the boom. The flat mounting surfaces are 1/4 inch wide. The long rod is 3/32 tube and the mounts are machined from solid brass stock. The length may be adjusted 5/8 inch and is locked with a #2-56 hex nut.
